> Akula Squadron

{% squadron_patch image="62nd_thumb" className="w-auto h-1/8" /%}

Akula Squadron earned its nickname, the Russian word for "shark", from its Soviet trainers to symbolize its predatory agility and relentless focus on air superiority. Comprised of several unique airframes, the Akula Squadron excels in its role of being a truly fierce interceptor squadron.

**Leadership Team:** TBD

**Primary Airframe:** MiG-29 Fulcrum

**Airframes**

- MiG-29 Fulcrum
- MiG-21 BIS
- Mirage F1
